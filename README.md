 ## BagSlotsFu

![Fubar BagSlotsFu](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/brujoloco/Images/refs/heads/main/Fubar1.png)

**BagSlotsFu** is a lightweight FuBar addon designed exclusively for **World of Warcraft 1.12 (Vanilla)**. It displays your **current free slots** and **total slots** in your bags. The addon is always on, ensuring efficient functionality. It also comes bundled with old FuBar-compatible libraries for seamless integration on WoW 1.12 private servers.

This addon is a perfect companion to **BankSlotsFu**, allowing players to monitor both their bag and bank space in tandem.

![Bags and Bank Slots in Fubar](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/brujoloco/Images/refs/heads/main/Fubar2.jpg
)

## Features
- **Bag Slot Tracking**: Displays the number of currently free slots and total slots in your bank.
- **Automatic Activation**: Activates and updates all the time.
- **FuBar Integration**: Bundled with old FuBar-compatible libraries for full compatibility with FuBar on WoW 1.12.
- **Companion Addon**: Designed to work perfectly alongside **BankSlotsFu** for complete inventory management.
- **Lightweight and Efficient**: Specifically optimized for WoW 1.12 without modern bloat.

## Installation
1. Download manually, or if your patcher allows it add the repository:
   ```bash
   https://github.com/brujoloco/BagSlotsFu.git

2. Let your patcher handle the install, select it on your patcher to enable it

3. If you prefer to do it manually move the BankSlotsFu folder to your WoW AddOns directory:

   World of Warcraft Folder \Interface\AddOns\

3. Start World of Warcraft and enable BankSlotsFu in the AddOns menu.

## Usage

BagSlotsFu will automatically display on your FuBar interface.

The addon will update to show:
Free Bag Slots: The number of empty slots available in your bank.
Total Bag Slots: The total number of slots in your bank.

Use it in tandem with BankSlotsFu to monitor both your bank and bag free slots


## Addon Pairing
BagSlotsFu is designed to be used alongside BankSlotsFu, located here: ![BankSlotsFu](https://github.com/brujoloco/BankSlotsFu)
but both addons can function independently. 

Together, they provide a complete overview of your inventory and bank space for efficient resource management.
